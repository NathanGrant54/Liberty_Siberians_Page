Meet Our Dogs

Gentry

UKC GCH/AKC MP Liberty's Country Boy at KPM
Gentry is a sweet boy and an all around dog, he will do anything you ask him to do from leading a sled team to deer tracking and dock diving. He has produced multiple champion offspring and will always be a member of our family.

Legacy

AKC MP Liberty's Living Legacy
Legacy is a SPUNKY boy! He is the "life of the party" type of dog, he makes his presence known. He is such a sweet boy, he started his show career March of 2022 and he is currently on the hunt for his last Major to finish his AKC CH title!

Mercy

UKC GCH/AKC PT Liberty's Have No Mercy
Mercy is a firecracker, she is your typical "crazy" husky, she has an amazing working drive and an off switch when needed but she likes to go full speed at all times! Mercy is a beautiful girl and we look forward to her bright future!

Monty

BIS AKC CH/UKC GCH Liberty's Good Ole Country Boy
Monty is son of Gentry and he is our up and coming young man that has exceeded all expectations! Monty finished his AKC CH title at only 11 months old, he finished his UKC GCH title in only 1 weekend! Monty is a happy go lucky boy with a heart of gold. Everyone that meets him remembers him and loves him!

Onyx

Liberty's Back in Black
Onyx is Zora's daughter and is new to our kennel, she is sweet as can be and loves to sit on your lap! Onyx is AKC pointed after just a couple shows and will be working towards her titles!
