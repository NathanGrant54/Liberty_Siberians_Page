About Us

We have a passion for Siberian Huskies. This has been building for 20 years and it seems we now, eat, sleep, live and breathe Siberian Huskies!! These amazing dogs start and end every day with us. We breed for structure, sound health and fabulous temperaments! We both show and sled with our dogs.

Bonnie Schaeffel and Steven Schaeffel, The founders of Liberty Siberians created a legacy worth continuing, Kenedi Wrezzes has taken over the Liberty Siberians kennel and hopes to build up and move forward with the beautiful animals entrusted to her!

About Bonnie and Steve- Bonnie is a strong willed beautiful woman who is firm on her beliefs and supportive and encouraging of those around her she is truly an amazing woman. Steve is the center of any room, his charismatic personality draws everyone to him and his heart of gold is unparalleled, Steve is truly a man who would give the shirt off his own back.

Together Bonnie and Steve embody Love, Commitment, and integrity. I have never met two people more committed to each other and the people around them in my life they are truly the best partners and mentors anyone could have asked for!

The Liberty Siberians team members have a common goal of breeding for improvement of the breed and encouraging people of all experience levels to try and enjoy dog sports of every kind!
